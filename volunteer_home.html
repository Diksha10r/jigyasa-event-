TREASURE HUNT




DATE : 31st MARCH 2018

REPORTING TIME : 9:30 AM

DURATION: 2 hours

RULES:

  • It will be a team event of one day. Each team should be of 4 members. There will be preliminary round, semifinal round and final round.
  • In preliminary round, there will be some set of tasks for each team.
  • A preliminary round will be of 15 minutes. Top 10 teams from preliminary rounds will qualify for the semi-final round.
  • Semi-Final round will be of again 15 minutes. Top 5 teams from semi-final round will compete for final round.
  • Final round will be of 30 minutes.The Team who will complete the task before other teams will be selected as the winning team.
  • In case, if no team is able to find the treasure, then team with maximum number of clues with solution will be selected as winner.
  • In case of any discrepancies (disclosing or destroying of clues etc.), the team will be disqualified.
  • Coordinators will reserve the rights to disqualify any team during the event in case of any discrepancies.
